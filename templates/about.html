Our Mission

To build the lives of vulnerable individuals by providing compassionate visits, empowering resources, and a strong support network that enables them to overcome adversity and thrive.

Our Vision

To create a society where every vulnerable person is embraced with hope, compassion, and empowerment needed to build a brighter future.

Our Story

From "My Little" to "Our Big Impact"

Founded on the belief that small acts of kindness accumulate to create massive change, the Better Homes Initiative (BHI) started as a grassroots movement. We identified critical gaps in education for children with special needs and healthcare for the elderly in rural Kenya.

Today, we work across multiple regions, renovating schools like Chepsogot School for the Blind and delivering sanitation essentials to homes in Kamariny.
